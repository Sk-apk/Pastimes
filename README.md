# Pastimes 👕

**Pastimes** is a web application that connects buyers and sellers of second-hand clothing in South Africa. The platform makes it easy to buy and sell pre-owned items that are still in good condition, while promoting sustainability and supporting social causes.

---

## About the Project

The rise of online shopping has created a strong demand for platforms that facilitate the exchange of pre-owned clothing. Pastimes addresses this by providing a secure, user-friendly marketplace where sellers can list items and buyers can browse and purchase them with confidence.

Beyond commerce, Pastimes integrates environmental transparency and charitable giving into its core model — making every purchase a meaningful one.

---

## Features

### For Buyers
- Browse and view pictures of available clothing items
- Communicate with sellers to ask questions or negotiate
- Add items to a shopping cart and edit selections before checkout
- Secure checkout with multiple shipping options
- Choose to purchase online or collect from a physical store

### For Sellers
- Submit clothing listings for admin review and approval
- Seller verification by admin before any items can be listed
- Real-time inventory management — sold items are removed promptly

### For Admins
- Approve or reject seller applications
- Create and manage clothing listings based on seller submissions
- Remove sold items from the database
- Communicate with both buyers and sellers
- Coordinate delivery between parties

### Platform Features
- **Sustainability Metrics Display** — visualises the environmental impact of clothing sold on the site, including carbon footprint data and eco-friendly packaging efforts
- **Charitable Partnerships** — a percentage of each purchase is donated to a charity of the buyer's choosing
- **Multi-Channel Sales** — online purchasing with information on physical store locations, inventory, and in-store promotions

## Database Overview

The database stores information across the following entities:

- **Buyer** — name, surname, email, password, delivery address, and payment details
- **Seller** — name, surname, email, password, and payment details
- **Clothing Item** — brand, size, colour, condition, and price
- **Delivery** — delivery time, date, location, and price
- **Admin** — name, surname, email, and password
